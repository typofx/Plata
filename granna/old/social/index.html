What is The Typo FX?

Deployed by Adam Soares in August 2013, the first focus was laptop computer maint and repair. Now we are coding and building up a software centre.

Our company

Typo FX Ltd. (Joke from the Portuguese accent, the sounds pronunce Tipo Fix) means Error Beep Sound or slang for error corresponding to syntax).
It was a second-hand and repair computer shop located in Dublin. The headquarter used to be at The D8 Arcade in Meath Street until the COVID-19 crisis. It is in Ronanstown Dublin 22 at the moment.

We had to rebuild ourselves after the "Corona Crash".
Even maintenance and repair are considered essential services, the volume of work had reduced significantly, giving the team more time to think, study and plan new challenges. Our team decided to put the first idea of many in action!
We have Deployed the first team's token on a blockchain. $TCU! This modern token is well known as TeuCurium, for everybody!

granna.ie
